Essential Compliance & Legal Portals

A simplified directory of resources used daily by our expert consultants.

Corporate Governance & Filing

  • Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)

    Official portal for all company formations, regulatory filings (ROC), and compliance forms.

  • National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)

    Tribunal for matters related to the Companies Act, Insolvency, and Mergers/Amalgamations.

  • Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

    Regulator for the securities market and compliance for listed companies.

Taxation & Finance

  • Income Tax Department

    Official portal for Income Tax, TDS, PAN, and e-filing services.

  • GST Portal

    The primary platform for Goods and Services Tax registration, returns, and compliance.

  • Central Board of Indirect Taxes & Customs (CBIC)

    Apex body for administering Customs, GST, and Central Excise matters.

Foreign Exchange & Banking

  • Reserve Bank of India (RBI)

    Central banking institution regulating monetary policy and FEMA compliance.

  • Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI)

    Regulator for the implementation of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), 2016.

Key Legal Acts & Legislation

  • Companies Act, 2013

    Full text of the core corporate law governing incorporation, director duties, and reporting.

  • India Code Portal

    Central repository of all Indian Union and State Laws (essential legal research tool).
