Corporate Law & Governance

We deliver seamless **corporate compliance and governance** solutions, protecting your directors and ensuring your operations align perfectly with the Companies Act and SEBI regulations.

Key Offerings:

  • Incorporation of Private and Public Limited Companies (PAN, TAN, DIN)
  • Ongoing Secretarial Services (Board Meetings, AGMs, Minutes)
  • Corporate Restructuring (Mergers, Amalgamations, Acquisitions)
  • Regulatory Filings with MCA, ROC, and SEBI
  • Advisory and representation in National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) matters

Taxation & Regulatory Compliance

Our tax expertise minimizes risk and maximizes your financial efficiency by ensuring absolute adherence to **GST and Income Tax** laws through proactive planning and timely filings.

Key Offerings:

  • GST Registration, Compliance, and Return Filings
  • Income Tax Planning and Advisory for Individuals and Corporates
  • Tax Audits and Representation before Tax Authorities
  • TDS/TCS Compliance and Filings
  • Statutory Annual Returns and Filings

Foreign Exchange & RBI Matters

We simplify complex cross-border transactions, providing authoritative advice on **FEMA, RBI, and international capital flows** to facilitate your global investment strategies and regulatory reporting.

Key Offerings:

  • Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Advisory and Compliance
  • Overseas Direct Investment (ODI) Advisory
  • External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) Documentation and Compliance
  • RBI Reporting and Approval Services (e.g., Annual Return on Foreign Liabilities)
