Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our services and operations.

Do you offer services to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)?

Yes, absolutely. While we handle complex matters for large enterprises, a significant portion of our practice is dedicated to providing specialized, scalable legal and compliance services for SMEs and startups to ensure a strong foundation for growth.

What is the typical timeline for forming a private limited company?

The timeline can vary based on regulatory approvals, but typically, once all necessary documents are prepared, the process takes approximately 7 to 14 business days, depending on the speed of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) processing.

Do you provide virtual or remote consultations?

Yes, due to our office address currently being vacant, all initial consultations and most advisory services are conducted remotely via video conference (Zoom, Google Meet) or telephone. This ensures convenience and efficiency for our clients globally.
